THE STORY OF BORJOMI LEGEND

Mineral water that rises from the earth in the depths of the Borjomi Gorge, surrounded by mountains, was first discovered by a wounded deer that entered the water and came out completely healed. The huntsman who witnessed the miracle also tasted the water and shared its rejuvenating powers with others.

THE DISCOVERY OF BORJOMI

The history of Borjomi begins with a legend, according to which the live water was first discovered by a wounded deer. The water healed the deer, and a hunter who witnessed the miracle tasted the water for himself, felt its rejuvenating power, and shared it with others. According to historical sources, the mineral waters of the Borjomi gorge date back to the first millennium AD, as revealed by the seven stone baths discovered at the beginning of the 20th century. Archeological findings show that in those times, Borjomi water was valued for drinking as well as bathing.However, this place was abandoned because of the 16th-17th century wars, and unfortunately, its valuable sources were forgotten.

HISTORY OF 130 YEARS

The legendary brand’s history has passed through many eras before ours; today, we can state that “Borjomi” is the face of Georgia. Along with changing historical periods, the visual side of the brand has also varied. However, what matters the most remains unchanged — the water’s distinct taste, unique mineral blend, and rejuvenating properties. Today, Borjomi is more than just ordinary mineral water to us all. It is also our national symbol representing the tradition of Georgian guest-loving nature and making us all proud.Let’s travel together in Borjomi’s world- look at its history of more than 130 years, feel the spirit of each era, and be proud as we honor our legend!
